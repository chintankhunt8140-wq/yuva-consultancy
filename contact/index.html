❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does IEC registration take?
With complete documents, IEC registration through the DGFT portal is typically completed within 1–3 working days. We handle the entire process for you.
Do I need to visit government offices?
No. All registrations are done online through official government portals. You can complete the entire process from your home or office with our remote assistance.
What documents are required for FSSAI?
FSSAI requires PAN, Aadhar, business registration proof, rent/ownership agreement, and food category details. We guide you through the exact documents needed for your type.
Can I register an international company from India?
Yes. We register companies in USA, UK, Dubai, Canada, Australia and Hong Kong completely remotely. No travel required — all done from Surat.
How long does ISO certification take?
Typically 30–60 days depending on your industry and current processes. ISO 9001 for small businesses can be completed in 30–45 days with our full support.
What is your fee for FSSAI registration?
Our consultancy fee is competitive and transparent with no hidden charges. Contact us for a free consultation and exact fee quote based on your FSSAI type needed.
