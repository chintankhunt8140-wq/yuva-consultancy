📖 Our Story

Building India's Businesses Since 2016

Yuva Consultancy was founded with a single mission: to make business registration and compliance simple, affordable, and stress-free for Indian entrepreneurs and exporters.

What began as a small consultancy in Surat has grown into a trusted partner for 1000+ businesses across India and internationally. We specialize in FSSAI, ISO certification, IEC, RCMC, ICEGATE, and international company registrations in USA, UK, Dubai, Canada, Australia, and Hong Kong.

Our team of expert consultants brings deep knowledge of Indian government portals, international business law, and export compliance to every client engagement.
